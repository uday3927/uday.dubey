How to Prepare Sandwiches

Sandwiches are easy to prepare and simply delicious in flavor when mixed with a lot of ingredients.

Vegetable cheese sandwich

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices bread (especially sandwich bread)
  • Butter
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Tomato (2 slices tomato required)
  • Several lettuce leaves
  • Several rings of sliced onion
  • Salt and pepper

Steps for making Vegetable Grilled Sandwich

  1. Apply Â½ tablespoon of butter on one side of bread.
  2. Place a slice of bread, butter side down onto the container.
  3. Add 1 or 2 slices of cheese on the top of the bread.
  4. Add ingredients like slices of tomato, lettuce leaves and sliced onion as a topping on the top of the cheese.
  5. Put the sandwich into the Grill Sandwich Toaster.
  6. Grill the sandwich until it starts to turn a light brown color.
  7. Leave it for another 2-3 minutes.
  8. Now, the sandwich is ready to eat.
  9. Use a spatula to keep the sandwich from Sandwich Toaster to Plate.
  10. Cut the sandwich into half and serve accordingly.
